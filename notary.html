MOBILE NOTARY

If you need a mobile notary in Washington, you’ll know by the language on your forms. At the end of the form, there should be a place for a notary’s signature and seal. Some documents call for a witness, and a notary can serve this purpose, too. Most documents and forms that are filed with the court require a notary.

Wills

Affidavits

Medical Directives

Structured Settlements

Taking Acknowledgements

Mortgage Closing Documents

Designations of Beneficiary

Administering Oaths and Affirmations

Witnessing or Attesting to a Signature (and the ID of the signer)

Affidavits of Independent Professional Advice

Our Notary Services puts you in contact with an on-demand Mobile Notary. Whether you’re looking for an emergency document to be signed after regular business hours, or you’d like a notary public to come to you, contact us for a professional and our direct services.

If you require assistance with your legal forms, we have references/referrals ready at your request to help you with your legal issues and needs.
