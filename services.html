SERVICES

Process Service

We can facilitate your needs as an uninterested third party. We hand deliver legal documents on behalf of Attorneys, Private Individuals, and Businesses.

Court Filings

We can file your documents for you whether you’re an Attorney, Private Individual, or Business. As with routine conventional filing, there is no extra charge for routine filing if it is in conjunction with our company providing service of process. If Rush filing is required, please call us for a price quote. Affidavits, and Supporting documents for the State of Washington with payment of the Filing Fee.

Skip Trace/Locate

We can assist you in the right search for the right task. Whether you need a general Skip Trace with an array of results, or a valid "Locate" with only validated results, or an infield locate with a Private Investigator to confirm residency, we are here to help.

Background Checks

Are you wanting to know who you are dealing with? Need to find out who you are dating, what sort of person lives next door? We can retrieve Background Checks on most individuals to put your mind at ease.

Court Records Search

Looking for a copy of your divorce decree? Maybe you want to review some criminal records for yourself or another individual? We can help. We will go to the courthouse, so you don't have to. We can also obtain copies from various State offices, including Business records for you.

Copy Services

Need help retrieving records? We have the ability to copy any sort of Medical Records, Private Records, Reports and more on-site at almost any location. We can also assist in records retrieval and printing services for these. No job is too much!

Private Investigator

Whether your case involves fraud, adultery, missing persons, child custody, extramarital affairs, an asset search to find out what someone has, our Private Investigator has the resources and expertise to find the answers you need.

Mobile Notary

Unable to find a Notary on call? Don’t worry, we can come to you. We have a Local traveling notary for the PNW area and surrounding areas.

If you require assistance with your legal forms, we have references/referrals ready at your request to help you with your legal issues and needs.
